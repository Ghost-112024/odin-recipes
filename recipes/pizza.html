Pizza recipe

Description

This is a quick and easy Pepperoni and Mushroom Pizza recipe to make when youre feeling lazy. If you get home from a long day feeling tired, and don't feel like cooking, this is a great thing to make!

Ingredients

Keep in mind that you can make a large, medium, small pizza of your choosing depending on your situation. For that reason, I will not list a specific amount of sauce, or cheese to use. Go with what works for you!

  • Pizza dough. (I use store bought, but if you know how to make your own, that's even better!)
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Set your oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Shred your cheese
  3. Slice your mushrooms
  4. Lay your dough on the baking sheet
  5. Apply sauce to dough
  6. Sprinkle your cheese over the sauce
  7. Lay pepperoni and mushrooms over the cheese
  8. Add one more light layer of cheese
  9. Put pizza in the oven
  10. Pull out your pizza when your cheese is getting golden brown
  11. Cut your pizza
Enjoy!
