B-L-T recipe

Description

This is quick and easy B-L-T recipe. It's simple but delicious, and is a great thing to make when you don't feel like washing lot's of pots and pans.

Ingredients

Please keep in mind that many of the ingredients on this list have many options to choose from. For example, there are many different flavors of bacon, and many kinds of lettuce and tomatoes. Choose what works best for you!

  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoess
  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onion
  • Mustard
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook your bacon in the oven, or airfyer to your desired crispness
  2. Prep the other ingredients while your bacon cooks
    • Cut tomatoes
    • Cut lettuce
    • Cut onions
    • Slice your cheese
    • Lay out your pickles
  3. Take your bacon out of the oven/airfryer
  4. Toast your bread
  5. Put together your sandwich
    • Butter your toast
    • Lay your cheese down on the toast
    • Apply Mustard
    • Add Bacon
    • Add Tomatoes
    • Add Pickles
    • Add Onion
    • Add Lettuce
    • Add top piece of toast
Your B-L-T is ready to eat. Enjoy!
