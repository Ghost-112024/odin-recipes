Alfredo recipe

Description

This is a delicioius Alfredo recipe, and it's simple enough that everyone can make it without much issue. It is a fairly simple dish, but it will surprise you how good it is!

Ingredients

You are free to add more spices if you'd like. The ones I have listed are a great foundation, but you can definitly add more! Just don't overdue it, as the parmesan itself is pretty salty.

  • Noodles 16oz. (I prefer fettucine, but feel free to use any noodle you like)
  • Parmesan 2 cups
  • Butter 1/2 cup
  • Heavy whipping cream 2 cups
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Mushrooms (as many as you like)
  • Parsley (as much as you like)
  • Salt 1/4 teaspoon
  • Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
  • Garlic powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Onion powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Italian seasoning 1/2 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil
  2. Add your noodles once your water is boiling
  3. Grate your parmesan, slice your mushrooms and and parsley while water boils
  4. Drain your noodles and let them rest
  5. Bring your sauce pan to a low medium heat
    • Add milk
    • Add butter
    • Add spices
  6. Stir untill your sauce starts to simmer
  7. Slowly add parmesan cheese while you whisk your sauce
  8. Once cheese has melted in
    • Add mushrooms
    • Add parsley
  9. Take sauce off the burner
  10. Grab a bowl, add your desired amount of noodles and sauce
Enjoy!
